About

Hello! I am Roger, a designer from Barcelona. In 2017 I got my Bachelor’s Degree in Industrial Design and Product Development at UPC. My studies and my professional career have given me knowledge and experience in all the phases of a creative process: the conceptualization, the design, the development and the product presentation. Also, I have experience in the graphic and interior fields.

I am a guy with constant concerns who ask himself why (too much) often. I like experimenting with the conceptualization and the making of products. I am fond of woodworking and I always have projects in mind (which I don’t have time nor space to spend in). I am also interested in design history and theories and I like to test them in small experiments.

I believe in a social and sustainable design as the path designers must take to move towards a better future. I advocate for the open design, open software and copyleft as basic tools. I also have a growing interest in the possible applications of artificial intelligence in design and the ethics debates around its use and the use of other technologies.
