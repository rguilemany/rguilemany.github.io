Enzo Mari 1123 xP

Experimentation | Product design
2018


In 1974 the Duchamp Centre edited and printed "Proposta per un'autoprogettazione" by Enzo Mari. The book, as it says in its introduction, is "A project for building of easy assembled furniture using rough boards and nails by those who will use them. A basic technique to let everyone view current production with a critical eye.".

To experiment with the concepts and techniques of it, I made a couple of 1123 xP chair.
